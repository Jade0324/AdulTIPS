# 🗺️ Product Roadmap: Adulting Survival Guide PH

This roadmap outlines the evolution of the system from a static guide to a "Personalized Adulting Operating System." The following phases are based on high-value feedback and market needs in the Philippines.

## 🟢 Phase 1: Core Framework (Current)
- [x] **Modular Architecture**: Reusable `ModuleTemplate` for rapid content deployment.
- [x] **Core Content**: Foundation, Finance, Property, Health, Living, and Growth modules.
- [x] **Local Persistence**: Checklist tracking via `localStorage`.
- [x] **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for the 18-30 Filipino demographic.

## 🟡 Phase 2: Personalization & Utility (Next Steps)
- **Personalized Roadmap Mode**: A discovery wizard (Age, Employment Status, Income) that filters modules into a custom path.
- **PH Cost Calculators**: 
  - Renting Calculator (Rent + Utilities + Commute).
  - Emergency Fund Calculator (Monthly Expenses × 3).
  - Car Ownership Hidden Cost Tracker.
- **Gov’t Process Wizards**: Decision-tree flows for Passport, NBI, and PhilSys applications.
- **Scam Awareness Module**: A dedicated `/protection` module covering online lending traps, GCash scams, and identity theft.

## 🟠 Phase 3: Engagement & Gamification
- **Progress Dashboard**: Visualizing "7/24 Adulting Essentials" completed.
- **Situation-Based Content**: "If This Happens..." blocks for crisis management (e.g., "Lost my ATM card," "Landlord won't return deposit").
- **Smart Reading Experience**: Estimated read times, "resume where you left off," and a progress bar per module.
- **PWA Support**: Full offline support for users with intermittent mobile data.

## 🔴 Phase 4: Intelligence & Scale
- **Adulting AI Assistant**: A custom-trained Gemini bot leveraging the module data to answer specific questions like "Should I rent or buy a condo?"
- **Admin CMS**: Lightweight integration with Supabase or Sanity to allow non-developers to update "What to Do" steps as government procedures change.
- **Partner Ecosystem**: Neutral, trust-based referrals to vetted insurance, banking, and legal services.

---

## 💡 Strategic Vision
The goal is to move from **Education** to **Execution**. By transforming theory into a "Situation-Based" solution engine, the platform becomes an indispensable tool for every young Filipino entering the workforce.
